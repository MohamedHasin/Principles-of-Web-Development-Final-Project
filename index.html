About This Event

HR Reinvented is an annual Human Resource discourse that brings together HR professionals from various industries to learn, share and exchange their thoughts with the panel of experts. HealthMetrics is proud to present you the 2022 edition that will be discussing on 'Reinventing the Workplace for the Future'.

We have come to an age where the workplace for the future is no longer bound by the traditional 9-5 system. For this year HR Reinvented 2022, we will dive deep into what is needed for us to be prepared for the future to ensure our people is ready for it.

Keeping it simple, this year format consist of 2 days that carries different sub-themes to capture the essence of future workplace. With more people coming back to work, while some are still working from home, each session is kept at 1 hour to ensure that the discussions will be precise and on-point.
